Usage: VOICE <channel> [user1] [user2] [...] [user15]
Voices the defined users on the channel:
 * channel - channel name
 * users   - the users to act on, up to 15 users.
If you do not provide a list of users, the bot will act on you.
Note: - the requester must hold OP privilege flag on the channel chanlev.
      - the bot will not warn if there are unknown nicks in the list
      - the bot will ignore authed nicks that may hold the DENYVOICE flag
End of help.
